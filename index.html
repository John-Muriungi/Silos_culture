Our Story

A Cool & Fine Place,
Always in Every Season

Silos Culture Mitunguu is more than a resort — it's a living, breathing cultural experience nestled in the heart of Meru County. Surrounded by lush greenery and open skies, it has been welcoming families, couples, and groups for years.

Every visit offers something special: the laughter of children in the park, the scent of mbuzi choma on the grill, the rustle of leaves along the nature trails, and the warmth of genuine Kenyan hospitality from our team.

Whether you're celebrating a milestone, planning a school trip, or simply need to breathe — Silos Culture is ready for you.

Find Us

Getting to
Silos Culture

We are located in Mitunguu, Meru County — easily accessible from Meru Town and the surrounding areas. The resort is well signposted once you enter Mitunguu.

  • From Nairobi: Take the Meru Highway (A2) to Meru Town, then continue to Mitunguu (~190 km, approx. 3.5 hrs)
  • From Meru Town: Head towards Mitunguu Market — Silos Culture is clearly signposted along the main road
  • By Matatu: Regular matatus from Meru Town to Mitunguu — ask to be dropped at Silos Culture Resort
  • GPS coordinates available on request — WhatsApp us for a pin drop
